Description:-
Valorant is a free-to-play first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for PC. First teased under the codename Project A in October 2019, the game began a closed beta period with limited access on April 7, 2020, followed by an official release on June 2, 2020. The development of the game started in 2014. Deginer of this game is Salvatore Garozzo and Trevor Romleski.
